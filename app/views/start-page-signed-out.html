Apply for maternity exemption certificates

If you are a midwife, GP or healthcare professional, use this online service to apply for maternity exemption certificates on behalf of expectant or new mothers.

A maternity exemption certificate entitles the mother to free prescriptions:

  • while pregnant
  • for 12 months after the birth of the child

Before you apply

You'll need the mother's:

  • date of birth
  • NHS number
  • due or birth date of their child
  • email or postal address

Apply for the Digital Maternity Exemption Service

If you are a midwife, GP or a health professional, use this online service to apply for maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

A maternity exemption certificate entitles the mother to free prescriptions:

  • while pregnant
  • for 12 months after the birth of the child

You'll need the mother's:

  • date of birth
  • NHS number
  • due or birth date of their child
  • email or postal address

Need more information about the digital MATEX service?

You can find more information about MATEX by clicking here.

Haven't received the certificate yet?

If applied through paper, note that the NHS BSA takes 7-10 working days to deliver your MATEX certificate.

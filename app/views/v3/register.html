Register for the Digital Maternity Exemption Service

Use this service to register to issue digital maternity exemption certificates.

You must be a midwife, practice nurse or health visitor registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) to create an account.

Need more information about the digital MATEX service?

You can find more information about MATEX by clicking here.

I'm a GP and would like to register

If you are a GP registered with the General Medical Council (GMC) you can still register to use this service.

To register, email nhsbsa.healthcosts@nhs.net and include your

  • name
  • GMC reference number
  • an email address with a secure service - such as NHSmail

Once we've received this information, we'll email you to confirm you are registered. You will then be able to use the online service.

What you'll need

To help us verify your professional identity you’ll need:

  • your NMC PIN
  • an email address with a secure service - such as NHSmail

Ask your IT department if your organisation’s email meets the secure email standard

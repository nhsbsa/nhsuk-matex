Do you know the mother's NHS number?

You do not need to provide the mother's NHS number, but it can help us match their details correctly.

This is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.
Where to find an NHS number

If the mother is registered with a GP, they will have a 10 digit NHS number unique to them.

It can be found on the NHS app or you can use our online NHS number finder (opens in a new tab).

It is also on any letters or documents the mother receives from the NHS, such as:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • appointment letters
  • maternity notes

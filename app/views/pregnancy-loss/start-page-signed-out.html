Apply for a maternity exemption

If you are a healthcare professional, you can complete an online application to issue maternity exemption certificates on behalf of expectant or new mothers.

A maternity exemption certificate entitles the mother to free NHS prescriptions and some free dental treatment.

Who is eligible?

The mother is eligible for a maternity exemption certificate:

  • if they are currently pregnant
  • for 12 months after the birth of a live child
  • if they have had a miscarriage
  • for 12 months after the birth of a child registered as stillborn

What if the mother has had a miscarriage or stillbirth?

The mother can continue to use their certificate for free NHS prescriptions.

The mother is still entitled to free NHS dental treatment if this happened during, or after, their 24th week of pregnancy.

You'll need the mother's:

  • date of birth
  • NHS number
  • due or birth date of their child
  • email or postal address

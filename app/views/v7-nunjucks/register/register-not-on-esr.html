Registering if you're not on ESR

If you're a midwife, practice nurse or health visitor, you can still use this service if you have an email address with an organisation that:

  • meets the secure email standard
  • uses Office 365 or a Hybrid Exchange email service

Ask your IT department if your organisation's email meets these requirements. We'll also check this for you.

To register, email at healthcosts@nhsbsa.nhs.uk and include your:

  • Full name
  • NMC PIN
  • organisational email address, such as NHSmail

We'll use these details to verify your professional identity.

We'll email you to confirm when you are registered. You'll then be able to use the online service.

Has the mother already given birth?
Extending the expiry date on the certificate

If the baby was born earlier than the expected due date, the mother can continue to use their existing MATEX certificate to claim free NHS prescriptions and dental treatment until it expires.

If the baby arrives after the expected due date the mother can send a copy of the birth certificate to the NHSBSA, once the baby is born, to extend the expiry date of their MATEX certificate.

For more info, visit the NHSBSA's knowledge base
